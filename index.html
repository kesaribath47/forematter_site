About

Automatic Aquarium Feeding and Peripheral Control

The Internet of Things (IoT) is the network of physical objects or "things" embedded with electronics to enable it to achieve greater value and service by exchanging data with the operator and/or other connected devices. The number of IoT devices are estimated to surpass the number of cellphones by the year 2021. Tomorrow’s Internet will be everywhere and in everything.

Forget to feed your aquarium fish? Nobody to feed them when you're not around? Or simply too lazy to feed them? Not a problem any more!

Introducing Cymba Aquarium Automation System which will take care of all your aquarium maintenance needs. As they say, just fit it and forget it.

Product Specs

    Aesthetically pleasing design to compliment the aquarium setup
    Storage capacity lasting for about a month for an average aquarium
    Easy to use and easy to install in almost all aquariums
    Powerful Wi-Fi antenna for easy connection to the local access point
    No battery worries, power adapter provided

    Internet enabled device, completely automated
    User-friendly and well designed Android app
    Change settings and monitor food level on the go using your mobile
    User profile creation: Supports multiple devices over the same profile
    Supports offline use also for users without Internet facility
